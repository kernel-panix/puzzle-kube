Explanation:

Currently working on a playable Rubik's Cube in CSS3/Javascript. After I get it fully functional, I'd like to add an auto-solving AI to the mix. I've been wanting to try my hand at this since I saw a team at The Nerdery in Chicago make one of these out for Google about 6 months ago.

Planned Features

  • Arrow keys to rotate the cube, and WASDQE for various rotation types. Ideally, I'll be switching over to mouse controls though.
  • Color-blind assistance mode via numbered colors. (As currently in the mock-up's primary face.)
  • Cube difficulty ranging from 2-7, the default being 3.
  • Cube auto-save so you can come back to it later.
  • Auto-scamble from solved state.
  • Auto-solve AI.
